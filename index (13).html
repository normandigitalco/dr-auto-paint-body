Common Questions

FAQs

Do you work with my insurance company? +
Yes — we work with all major insurance carriers including State Farm, GEICO, Allstate, Progressive, USAA, Farmers, Liberty Mutual, and more. We handle the entire claims process so you don't have to make calls or chase paperwork.
How long will my repair take? +
Repair time depends on the extent of damage and parts availability. Minor dent or bumper repairs typically take 2–4 days. Collision repairs with structural damage typically range from 1–2 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline upfront and update you throughout the process.
Do you use OEM or aftermarket parts? +
We prefer OEM (original equipment manufacturer) parts to ensure proper fit and function. If your insurance specifies aftermarket parts, we'll discuss your options and always prioritize quality and safety.
Is your work guaranteed? +
Yes. All of our repairs come with a written workmanship guarantee. If something isn't right after you pick up your vehicle, bring it back and we'll make it right — no questions asked.
Can I get a rental car while my vehicle is being repaired? +
If the accident was covered by insurance, your policy likely includes rental reimbursement. We can help coordinate rental arrangements through your insurer. We're also conveniently located near several rental car providers in McKinney.
